ROWNS 3D Lane + Pedestrian Study

This version uses proper lanes, crosswalks, sidewalks, multiple intersection vehicles, and pedestrians. The ego vehicle stays in the right lane, cross traffic moves in correct perpendicular lanes, and ROWNS yields for both cars and people.

  • Yield for cross traffic, pedestrians, or blocked crosswalks.
  • Continue once the conflict zone and crosswalk are clear.
  • The simulator logs reaction time, yield behavior, collisions, pedestrian conflicts, and lane position.
