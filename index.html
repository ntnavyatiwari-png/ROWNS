ROWNS Interface Comparison Simulator

The study compares different ways of showing right-of-way guidance under more ambiguous intersection situations.

  • Baseline: no ROWNS output.
  • Text panel: GO/YIELD with explanation and confidence.
  • Minimal signal: only a large GO/YIELD cue.
  • Spatial overlay: colored road zone showing where the conflict is.
Task

Drive toward the stop line. Yield when the situation is unsafe, then continue when clear. Some scenes include distractor traffic, delayed pedestrians, and staggered cars.
